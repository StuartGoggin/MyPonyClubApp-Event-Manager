# How to Nominate Your Club Committee

## Quick Guide for Pony Club Committee Nominations

### Before You Begin

✓ Hold your Annual General Meeting (AGM)  
✓ Elect your committee members at the AGM  
✓ Have the AGM minutes available (PDF format, max 5MB)  
✓ Gather contact details for all committee members:
  - Full names
  - Pony Club ID numbers
  - Email addresses
  - Mobile phone numbers

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Visit the Nomination Page
Go to: **www.myponyclub.com.au/nominate-committee**

### 2. Select Your Club
- Choose your Pony Club from the dropdown list
- Your Zone will be automatically displayed
- Click **Continue**

### 3. Enter AGM Details
- **AGM Date**: Select the date your Annual General Meeting was held
- **AGM Minutes** (Optional): Upload your AGM minutes PDF file

### 4. Nominate District Commissioner (Required)
- Search for and select your District Commissioner
- The system will auto-fill their contact details
- Verify email and mobile number are correct
- **Important**: This nomination requires approval from your Zone Representative

### 5. Add Core Committee Positions (Optional)
Complete as applicable:
- **President**
- **Vice President**
- **Secretary**
- **Treasurer**

For each position:
- Search by name or Pony Club ID
- Contact details will auto-fill
- Verify information is correct

### 6. Add Additional Committee Members (Optional)
- Click **Add Committee Member**
- Enter position title (e.g., "Equipment Officer", "Chief Instructor")
- Search and select the person
- Repeat for all additional members

### 7. Submitter Information (Required)
Enter your details as the person submitting this nomination:
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Phone Number

### 8. Additional Notes (Optional)
Add any relevant information about your committee

### 9. Review and Submit
- Check all information is correct
- Click **Submit Committee Nomination**
- You will receive a confirmation on screen

---

## What Happens Next?

1. **Zone Representative Review**  
   Your Zone Representative will review the District Commissioner nomination

2. **Approval or Feedback**  
   They will either approve the nomination or request changes

3. **Email Notification**  
   You will be notified via email of the decision

4. **Edit if Needed**  
   You can edit or withdraw the nomination before it's approved

---

## Need Help?

**Having Trouble?**
- Ensure you have JavaScript enabled in your browser
- Use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
- Clear your browser cache if the form doesn't load properly

**Can't Find Someone?**
- Make sure they have a Pony Club ID in the system
- Check the spelling of their name
- Contact your Zone Representative for assistance

**Questions?**
Contact your Zone Representative or State Office for support

---

## Important Notes

⚠️ **One Committee Per Year**: Each club can only have one active committee nomination per year

⚠️ **District Commissioner Approval**: The DC nomination must be approved by your Zone Representative before it becomes official

⚠️ **Keep Information Updated**: Ensure all contact details are current so committee members receive important communications

⚠️ **AGM Must Be Held First**: You cannot nominate a committee before holding your AGM
